STEP 7: EPISTEMIC SELF-ASSESSMENT
Assess the quality of your own reasoning. Report two scores honestly:

reasoning_quality — How sound is the logical structure of your deliberation?
  0.0 = reasoning is circular, unsupported, or jumps to conclusions
  0.5 = reasoning is plausible but has gaps or unverified assumptions
  1.0 = rigorous step-by-step logic, every claim traceable to evidence
  Ask: if someone examined my reasoning chain, would it hold up?

outcome_certainty — How clearly does the available evidence support
  the recommended action over alternatives?
  0.0 = evidence is contradictory or absent; multiple outcomes equally plausible
  0.5 = evidence leans toward the recommendation but alternatives are plausible
  1.0 = evidence unambiguously supports this outcome; no credible alternative

  a different conclusion?
